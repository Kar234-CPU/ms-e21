MS Energy E21 Electric Scooter

The MS Energy E21 is a modern electric scooter designed for urban commuting. It offers a blend of performance, safety, and convenience for everyday riders.

Key Features

  • Max speed: 25 km/h
  • Range: up to 90 km per charge
  • Motor power: 500W
  • Battery: 36V, 20.8Ah
  • Weight: 25 kg
  • Max weight: 100kg (>100kg)
  • Charging time: 12~ hours
  • Braking system: Front and rear disc brakes
  • Tires: 10-inch pneumatic tires
  • Display: LCD with speed and battery status
  • IP54 water resistance rating
  • Hard aluminum frame
  • Front and rear lights for safety
  • Foldable design for easy storage
  • LCD display for speed and battery status

The E21 is perfect for long trips, daily commutes, and eco-friendly travel.
